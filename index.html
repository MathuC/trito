Trito











Instructions

Goal: Dodge the rectangles coming your way.

A: Square to the left
S: Square to the middle
D: Square to the right
J: Half-squares to the left and to the middle
K: Half-squares to the left and to the right
L: Half-squares to the middle and to the right
Space: Trisected squares to all the positions


~ This game aims at testing and challenging your reaction time ~
